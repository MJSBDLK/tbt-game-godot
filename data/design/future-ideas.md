## Character Concepts - Fourth Crew Member

Context: Ma'am, Ernesto, and Max are the starting three. We already have planned recruits (Ogre Squire, Sniper+Squig, Elf Pirate, Gravity Commander). These are explorations for additional character concepts.

### Drama-Creating Character
- A character who is an "endless source of drama"
- Creates interesting conflict by putting "funny ideas" into crew's heads
- Example: Max getting upset over something minor because this character "poisoned the well"
- Sneaky personality, manipulator type
- Could test other characters' relationships and principles

**Pros:** Creates organic character-driven conflict, comedy potential
**Cons:** Could be annoying if overused, requires careful writing to avoid being unlikable
**Scope concern:** HIGH - requires complex dialogue systems and relationship tracking

### Grandpa Figure
- Too old to help physically
- Welcomes his own death (morbid but interesting character trait)
- Wisdom/mentor role after Ma'am dies

**Pros:** Different energy from existing cast, mentor replacement
**Cons:** Similar role to late Ma'am, death-wish character might be too dark
**Scope concern:** MEDIUM - simpler to implement than drama character

### Second Mother Figure / Disabled Ogre
- Joins squad around midgame
- "Base mom" archetype
- Possibly an injured/disabled ogre warrior

**Pros:** Fills Ma'am's void differently, ogre connection interesting
**Cons:** Another mother figure might feel repetitive, disability representation requires care
**Scope concern:** MEDIUM - depends on how disability affects mechanics
